**Human cognitive energy follows circadian rhythms, ultradian cycles, and personal patterns.** Most people have:

- Predictable energy dips (post-lunch, late afternoon)
- Unique personal energy signatures

**Current solutions are broken:**
- ❌ Generic "time blocking" ignores energy reality
- ❌ No feedback loop between energy and outcomes
- ❌ No gamification to sustain behavior change
