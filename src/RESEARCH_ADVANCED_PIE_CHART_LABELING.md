### 4. **Accessibility in Data Visualization (W3C WAI, 2023)**

**Guideline: "Making Data Visualizations Accessible"**

**Requirements for Accessible Legends:**

1. **Keyboard Navigation**
   - Legend items must be keyboard accessible
   - Tab order should be logical

### 8. **Animation & Motion Design (Google Material Design, 2024)**

**Study: "Motion in Data Visualization"**

**Best Practices:**

1. **Duration**
   - Micro-interactions: 100-200ms
   - Transitions: 200-300ms
   - Complex animations: 300-500ms
   - Never exceed 500ms

2. **Easing**
   - Enter: `ease-out` (decelerating)
   - Exit: `ease-in` (accelerating)
   - Both: `ease-in-out` (smooth)
   - Avoid linear (feels robotic)

3. **Purpose**
   - Feedback: Confirm user action
   - Relationship: Show connections
   - Hierarchy: Guide attention
   - Continuity: Maintain context

**Chart Interaction Animation:**
